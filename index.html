About me

Hi, I'm Maya 👋

I'm a product designer based in San Francisco, originally from Vancouver. I care a lot about making software feel genuinely good — not just functional, but warm and trustworthy.

Before design, I studied cognitive science, which shapes how I think about mental models, decision-making, and why people do what they do. These days I'm focused on health and fintech products where good design genuinely changes people's lives.

Outside of work I'm usually hiking, making very mediocre pottery, or testing new sourdough recipes.
